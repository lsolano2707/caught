Subject Pronouns

When the person or thing is the subject of the sentence, subject pronouns are used.

Example:

I I live in Colombia Yo vivo en Colombia
You You play soccer in the park Tu juegas futbol en el parque
He He eats pizza El come pizza
She She is tall Ella es alta
It It is my dog Este es mi perro
We We go to the school Nosotros vamos a la escuela
You You run in the park Ustedes corren en el parque
